The Problem: Fragmented Support for Founders

Founders navigate a complex ecosystem with no single, integrated platform to streamline their journey from idea to funding.

  • Disconnected Ideation & Collaboration: No unified space to ideate, build concepts (problem, solution, market), and manage shared collateral (spreadsheets, PPTs) securely with co-founders.
  • Complex Company Formation & IP: Tedious, manual processes for company registration, DPIIT/MSME recognition, and ensuring brand name/trademark availability and protection.
  • Opaque Funding & Equity Management: Difficulty understanding diverse funding options (VCs, angels, govt. grants) and simulating equity dilution scenarios for different valuations.
  • Compliance Overload: Constant deadlines for GST, IT, PF, and other regulatory filings, leading to penalties if missed, without centralized alerts or assistance.

The Solution: Startup Saathi – Your All-in-One Founder OS

An intelligent, secure, and integrated web portal empowering startups across their entire lifecycle. This is a global solution, applicable to startups worldwide.

Unified Workspace: A secure environment for all your startup groundwork, from idea to execution.

Intelligent Tools: AI-powered insights for funding, compliance, and strategic planning.

Collaborative Environment: Securely share and manage work with your co-founders and team.

Expert-Curated Resources: Templates, guides, and network access for informed decision-making.

Objective Idea Score: Data-driven assessment based on concept, team, market, and investor alignment.

MVP: The "Groundwork Launchpad" (2-3 Months)

Our immediate focus is to provide a secure, intuitive, and collaborative space for founders to define their idea, formalize their company, and get initial funding/equity clarity.

Functions We Want to Cover

  • Secure User Accounts & Collaboration: Registration/login, dashboard, basic team invitation, shared workspace.
  • Concept Builder: Structured sections (Problem, Solution, Product, Market, Revenue, Traction, Team, Ask, Vision) with AI Integration (Idea Sparker, Content Assistant, Executive Summary Draft).
  • Basic File Repository: Upload and view common documents.
  • Company Registration & Essential Templates: Guided info on incorporation (Pvt Ltd, LLP, OPC), DPIIT/MSME. AI Integration: Document Checklist, Template Explainer. Downloadable templates (MoA, AoA, Founders Agreement, etc.).
  • IP & Branding Checks: Simulated Name/Trademark & Domain Checks. AI Integration: Brand Name Generator, Trademark Insight.
  • Curated Government Funding Options: List of key Indian grants/schemes with summaries and official links. AI Integration: Scheme Summarizer, Relevance Suggestor.
  • Base Valuation & Equity Allocation Simulator: Interactive tool to input current equity, simulate a single funding round, and basic dilution impact. AI Integration: Multiple Contextualizer, Dilution Explainer.

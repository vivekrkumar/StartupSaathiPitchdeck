The Problem: Navigating the Startup Maze

Indian founders face a unique set of challenges that divert focus from core innovation.

  • Company Registration & Legal Access: Lack of instantaneous access to CA/CS for formation details, essential templates, and quick company name availability checks.
  • Name & Brand Sanitization: Uncertainty about the availability of company names and brands, leading to potential trademark infringement risks.
  • Fragmented Workspace: No dedicated online workspace to centralize initial ideas, workings, and foster seamless collaboration among co-founders.
  • Idea-to-Pitch Conversion: Difficulty in quickly structuring and converting nascent ideas into professional, investor-ready pitch decks.
  • Capital Raise & Dilution Complexity: Lack of clarity on how to structure capital raises and insufficient simulation options to understand equity dilution impacts.
  • Limited Funding Awareness: Founders are often unaware of available government programs, grants, and various funding options specifically for early-stage startups.
